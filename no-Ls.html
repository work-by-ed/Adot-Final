No L's - Adot Prod. Machobeatz

Fresh off of the stage at the first annual Lyrical Lemonade Summer Smash just a few days back, LL favorite & Chicago native is back with a brand new tune titled “No L’s”. He joined forces with producer Macho Beatz who cooked up this menacing instrumental, and if you know anything about Adot you know that he thrives over these types of beats, so you can already assume the outcome. It’s been awesome to watch Adot grow as an artist since 2014, he has come a far way but I think that he is still in the blossoming stages, leaving only the best to come.
